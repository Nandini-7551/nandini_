Key Data Insights
📈
CGPA is the #1 predictorStudents ≥8.5 CGPA place at 79.1% vs 18.1% below 7. The gap is stark — every decimal matters.
🎯
Aptitude is the surprise factorScoring 80-90 on aptitude is 8× better than 50-60 (65.8% vs 2.6%). Practice mock tests religiously.
🏆
Extracurriculars = 4.5× multiplier62% placed with activities vs 13.7% without. Clubs, competitions, volunteering — anything counts.
💼
2 internships is the magic numberJumps from ~33% (0 or 1) to 69.7% with 2 internships. The second one is disproportionately valuable.
📚
Placement training triples odds51.6% trained vs 15.6% untrained. Single most actionable institutional intervention.
